* Support Vector Machines (SVM)

  In this article, I will try to cover the mathematics and intuition
  behind one of the classic Machine Learning algorithms, Support
  Vector Machines, abbreviated as SVM. This algorithm, unlike linear
  regression, is used to classify data into differnet classes. For
  example, whether a tumor is malignant or benign, whether or not a
  customer will pay back loans to the bank, and did I do your mum last
  night, etc. are some examples of classification problem.  The
  classification problem involving two classes is called a binary
  classification problem, whereas the ones involving more than two are
  referred to as multiclass classification problem.

  In its most simple form, SVM does not support multiclass
  classification. It supports binary classification only. So, without
  further ado, let us understand the algorithm.
